Dronesperhour wants help enable the emerging drone ecosystem by connecting pilots and projects in a simple and transparent way

The Platform

We are your drone marketplace, helping you navigate a new emerging market with open eyes.

Dronesperhour is about honesty and market transparency and we can help companies traverse the new emerging market of drone services. Contact us and we can always advise you on both the tech side of project elements and also which pilots and drones may be the best fit for you. We can also help companies post their own projects onto the website to quickly get bids so that you can quickly get to work.

The Vision

From the seed of an idea to build a transparent platform for pilots to connect with companies, we started to build a community by pilots for pilots.

It’s easy to get caught up in the rush and excitement of both the business and the emerging drone tech market, but we never wanted to forget about our beginnings. Honesty has always been at the front of what we do and always will be, because behind the tech is always a person. We fly because we believe we can help and educate those around us. The future is now. With film and photography costs becoming cheaper with drones, and advanced data analytics from these images making the world smarter, together we’ve all come a long way in a short time. This isn’t just a big moment for us here at Dronesperhour, or even in our industry, this is one of the small steps that lead to another giant leap for us all. It’s a brave new world, come fly with us.




Braunwald

a hidden, beautiful ski resort in the heart of the Glarner country in Switzerland. The region showed itself with beautiful Kaiserwetter, optimal conditions for our DJI Inspire to capture the panorama.




Campingplatz Platen

Campsite Platen is a campsite in Sehlendorf, in the heart of Schleswig-Holstein, on the edge of the Holstein-Switzerland and directly on the Ostsee. Just take a look how nice camping at the Ostsee could be.




Stubbenfelde

Camping directly behind the dunes. Campsite Stubbenfelde is a campsite in Stubbenfelde, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, located by the sea and in a wood. Right behind there is a Ostsee with beautiful fine and long sandy beach.




Campingplatz Surcuolm

The Campsite Surcuolm in Obersaxn (Graubünden) offers peace and relaxation for the whole family and is the ideal starting point for a wide range of excursions and activities in the surrounding area. Discover the beauty and the variety of the offer of this region.
